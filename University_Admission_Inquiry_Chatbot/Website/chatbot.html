Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements for undergraduate programs?

For undergraduate programs, you need a Higher Secondary School Certificate (HSSC) or equivalent with at least 45% marks. Additional requirements may vary by program.

When is the application deadline for Fall 2023?

The application deadline for Fall 2023 is August 30, 2023. We recommend applying early to ensure all documents are processed on time.

How can I check my application status?

You can check your application status through our online portal. You'll need your application reference number to access your status.

Does the university offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer various scholarships based on merit and financial need. Please visit our financial aid office for more information.
