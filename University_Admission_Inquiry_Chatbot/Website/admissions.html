Admission Requirements

Requirements for Undergraduate Programs

  • Completed application form with all required information
  • Higher Secondary School Certificate (HSSC) or equivalent with minimum 45% marks
  • Copy of CNIC/B-Form
  • Two recent passport-size photographs
  • Original bank challan of admission processing fee
  • Character certificate from last institution attended
  • Migration certificate (for students from other boards)

Requirements for Graduate Programs

  • Completed application form with all required information
  • Bachelor's degree with minimum 2.5/4.0 CGPA or 50% marks
  • Official transcripts from all institutions attended
  • Copy of CNIC
  • Two recent passport-size photographs
  • Original bank challan of admission processing fee
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose (for selected programs)

Requirements for International Students

  • Completed application form with all required information
  • Equivalent qualification certificates attested by IBCC
  • Copy of passport
  • Student visa (after admission approval)
  • Two recent passport-size photographs
  • Proof of English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS) if applicable
  • Health certificate including HIV test report
  • Financial support documents
