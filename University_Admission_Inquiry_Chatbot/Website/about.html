Our History & Mission

The University of Sargodha was established in 2002, building upon the legacy of the Government College Sargodha which was founded in 1929. Over the years, the university has grown to become one of Pakistan's premier institutions of higher education.

Our journey has been marked by academic excellence, research innovation, and a commitment to serving the community. From humble beginnings, we've expanded to offer a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across multiple disciplines.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of higher education recognized for academic excellence, research innovation, and societal impact.

Our Mission

To provide quality education, foster research, and develop skilled professionals who contribute to national development.

About the Project

The Admission Inquiry Chatbot is an AI-powered virtual assistant developed using Rasa framework to handle student inquiries about admissions, programs, and university services. The chatbot is integrated with this responsive university website to provide seamless support to prospective students.

Technologies Used: Rasa, Python, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
